## üö® Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
**Risk:** System downtime during critical deals
**Mitigation:** 
- 99.9% uptime SLA with hosting provider
- Automated backups every 6 hours
- Disaster recovery plan
- Status page for transparency

### Competitive Risks
**Risk:** Competitor launches similar tool
**Mitigation:**
- Continuous innovation (monthly feature releases)
